I love capturing the magic
in people through my camera

I fell in love with photography a long time ago, when I was studying art. The dramatic chiaroscuro in Caravaggio's paintings, the way he used light and the way he shown beauty in the ordinary, were very inspirational to me. I could never have painted like that, but I try to find the beauty in everyone that I photograph.

My knowledges about art, aesthetic and graphic design have been very important in my carrer as a photographer. After all, it's seems like I have been preparing myself for this amazing profession all my life.
So if you are looking for a photographer who could capture a timeless portraits you can cherish forever, you are in the right page.
