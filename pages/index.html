My name is Yudi Ramos,
and I love Photography.

I truly think Photography is most definitely art! It's about capturing the emotion and the beauty of the moment.

My style is a combination between portrait photography and fine-art photography with a touch of fashion and creative lighting. My photos are inspired by light, color, techniques from black & white processing, vintage photos, creative perspective, and of course, most importantly, the personalities of the people I photograph!
