INVESTMENT

I fell in love with photography a long time ago, when I was studying art. The dramatic chiaroscuro in Caravaggio's paintings, the way he used light and the way he shown beauty in the ordinary, were very inspirational to me. I could never have painted like that, but I try to find the beauty in everyone that I photograph.
